1. Overview

VP Bank (short for ValuePlus Bank) is a full-service commercial and retail bank headquartered in Bengaluru, Karnataka, India.
Established in 2005, VP Bank has rapidly grown into a trusted financial institution with over 180 branches and 250 ATMs across India.

The bank is known for its technology-driven customer experience, offering a blend of traditional banking services and modern digital platforms to individuals, small businesses, and corporate clients.

VP Bank’s motto is:

“Your Trust, Our Value.”

9. Customer Queries and Information

Q1: How can I open a savings account with VP Bank?
A: You can open an account online via the VP Mobile app or visit any branch with valid KYC documents (Aadhaar, PAN, and proof of address).

Q2: Does VP Bank provide home loans?
A: Yes, VP Bank offers home loans up to ₹2 crore with flexible repayment options up to 25 years.

Q3: Is there an app for mobile banking?
A: Yes, download the VP Mobile App on Android or iOS to access all banking services anytime.

Q4: How can I block my debit card if it’s lost?
A: You can block your card instantly via the mobile app, by sending “BLOCK CARD” on WhatsApp to +91 90000 88888, or by calling 1800-202-8888.

Q5: What is the minimum balance for a savings account?
A: ₹5,000 for metro branches, ₹2,000 for semi-urban branches, and ₹1,000 for rural branches.

Q6: Does VP Bank provide investment or mutual fund services?
A: Yes, through the VP Wealth platform, offering mutual funds, bonds, insurance, and portfolio management.
